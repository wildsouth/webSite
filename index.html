about us

What makes our food special?

It’s the way we grind our spices fresh, slow-cook our stews and prepare each meal with care, just like it’s done in a true Nigerian home. Our recipes are rooted in generations of knowledge, yet made fresh for today’s taste.

We believe food should be flavorful, nourishing and unforgettable. That’s why every plate from Food by South bursts with authenticity from smoky jollof rice and tender goat meat, to velvety egusi and soft, fluffy pounded yam.

Whether you're a local foodie or tasting Nigerian dishes for the first time, you’ll feel the warmth and pride in every bite.
